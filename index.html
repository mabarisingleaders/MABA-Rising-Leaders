ABOUT US

MABA RISING LEADER’S MONTESSORI MODEL SCHOOL (MRMMS) is an educational institution that provides holistic training for children to achieve social, physical, spiritual, and academic excellence, in a spacious and conducive environment through the fear of God, quality teaching, and supervision.

Our educational philosophy of nurturing young brains and enhancing talents is driven by firm Christian values which embody integrity, Discipline, Morality, Excellence, Leadership, and Quality Service Delivery.

PARTNERSHIP WITH STAKEHOLDERS

The success of learners largely depends on the unlimited support of parents and all stakeholders. We depend largely on the partnership of parents to unleash the “adult” in each child. We maintain clear communication between parents, teachers, and management. Parents will be involved in meetings at the beginning and end of every academic session to discuss expectations and evaluate performance.
